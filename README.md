# ☕ Monday Coffee – SQL Data Analysis Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the sales data of **Monday Coffee**, an online coffee store that launched in **January 2023**.
The objective is to use **SQL-based data analysis** to identify the **best cities in India to expand the coffee business** by examining sales trends, customer behavior, and market potential.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The company wants to determine **which cities are the most suitable for opening new coffee shop locations**.

Using SQL queries, this project evaluates:

* Customer demand
* Revenue generation
* Market size
* Rent vs revenue potential
* Product performance

The final goal is to recommend **the top cities for business expansion**.

---

## 🗂 Dataset Tables

The project uses four main datasets:

1. **City**

   * City name
   * Population
   * Estimated rent
   * City rank

2. **Customers**

   * Customer ID
   * Customer name
   * City ID

3. **Products**

   * Product ID
   * Product name
   * Price

4. **Sales**

   * Sale ID
   * Sale date
   * Product ID
   * Customer ID
   * Total sale amount
   * Rating

---

## 📊 Key SQL Analysis

The following business questions were solved using SQL:

1. Coffee Consumers Count by City
2. Total Revenue from Coffee Sales (Q4 2023)
3. Sales Count for Each Product
4. Average Sales Amount per City
5. City Population vs Coffee Consumers
6. Top Selling Products by City
7. Customer Segmentation by City
8. Average Sale vs Rent Analysis
9. Monthly Sales Growth by City
10. Market Potential Analysis

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### Pune

* Highest total revenue
* High average sales per customer
* Low rent per customer

### Delhi

* Highest estimated coffee consumers
* Large customer base

### Jaipur

* High number of customers
* Very low rent per customer

---

## 🏆 Final Recommendation

The **top 3 cities recommended for expansion** are:

1️⃣ Pune
2️⃣ Delhi
3️⃣ Jaipur

These cities show **strong customer demand, high revenue potential, and favorable cost conditions**.
